Definitions

o "Own Goal": Goal is scored on team "A", having a player from team "A" be the last to touch the puck. (Golie not included)

o "Lost Fan": A fan in the arena is seen wearing a jersey that does not belong to either the Home or Away team.

o "Double-Double": Two players wearing the same jersey number are on the ice at the same time.

o "Rookie": A a player who has yet to complete a full NHL season is on the ice during play.

o "Blood!": One or more players on the ice is bleeding.
